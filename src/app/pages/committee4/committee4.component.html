Rakshak Bharath is a final year student of Mechanical Engineering at MVSR Engineering College, Hyderabad. He has participated in over 20 Model United Nations conferences in the capacity of a delegate, Organising Committee member and Executive Board member. He also served as the Secretary General for The International Thematic Conference 2018. He takes profound interest in studying and analysing the effects of policy and its spill over effects. He believes that critical thinking and a thirst for knowledge are prized assets to posses and help not only with MUNs but everyday life as well.
