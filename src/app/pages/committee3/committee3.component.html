About

Universal, incontrovertible, subjective: serving as a guardian of these principles of human rights in these places close to home, the United Nations Human Rights Council upholds the rights that are implicit in individuals simply by virtue of their being human. At IITM-MUN 2019, we are proud to present it as one of the five councils what will be simulated at the conference.

The UN Human Rights Council is an inter-governmental body that was formed on the 15th of March, 2006. It is a subsidiary body of the UN General Assembly that comprises 47 member states, with a mandate to strengthen the protection and promotion of human rights across the globe, address situations of violations, and make recommendations on them. It has the ability to discuss all thematic human rights issues and situations that require its attention throughout the year. It also promotes the implementation of human rights obligations undertaken by States and helps them in building dialogue, providing technical assistance, and capacity building measures.
