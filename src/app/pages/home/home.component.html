The human voice is the most powerful instrument of all – it can start wars, it can spread peace. For the past three years, the students of IIT Madras have been proud to play host to an assemblage of the brightest academic and professional minds from across the country at the IITM Model United Nations Conference. Model UN conferences are highly intensive academic simulations of the conclaves of the United Nations, or other governmental and international bodies, which introduce students to the world of diplomacy, negotiation, and decision making. With consistent participation of over 200 delegates over the years, in the fourth iteration of the conference in 2019, we plan to build upon this foundation and provide wide fora for participants to engage with their peers in policy-oriented debate and deliberations on international law and affairs. We firmly believe in the active participation of students in the policy formulation processes that have worldwide impact.
